Chamaecyparis lawsoniana 'Pembury Blue' - Lawson's Cyprus

Description

A genus of the cyprus, these trees are a hardy selection of evergreen conifers. This variety is a medium-sized conical tree with swathes of silvery-blue floiage. A striking cultivar and handsome Lawson Cypress. Thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Suggested Uses

Ornamental trees of hedging

Size

From 5 to 15 feet high
