A more detailed description of this genus of the evergreen coniferous family of trees


Chamaecyparis lawsoniana 'Columnaris Glauca' - Lawson's Cypress

Description

A genus of the cyprus, these trees are a hardy selection of evergreen conifers. This variety is a deep blue-green tree, hardy and not easily disturbed by movement. They thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Suggested Uses

Ornamental trees or hedging

Size

From 5 to 15 feet high
