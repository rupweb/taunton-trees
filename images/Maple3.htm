Acer japonicum 'Aureum' - Japanese Maple

Description

An elegant large deciduous shrub or small tree. The leaves turn from bright green in spring to a rich orange and red in autumn. The flowers are help in cream petals with pink calyces and grow in clusters.

Suggested Uses

For autumn colour and flowers

Size

From 8 feet high
