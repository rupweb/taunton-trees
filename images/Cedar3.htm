Cedrus Deodara - Cedar

Description

Renowned for grandeur and longevity, these trees develop massive trunks and branches as they age. The narrow needle-like leaves spiral around in rosettes. The deodara is a most beautiful and large tree with bluish leaves when young, maturing to a deep green. Good drainage is essential, and cedars will grow on all kinds of soil from acid sands to clays and chalk.

Suggested Uses

A familiar and picturesque setting.

Size

6-10 feet although growing to 50 feet high
