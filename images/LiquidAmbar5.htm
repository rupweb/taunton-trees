A more detailed description of these deciduous maple-like trees


Liquidambar styraciflua - Sweet Gum

Description

A large deciduous tree, beautiful at all times but especially in autumn when the previously green leaves form their crimson colouring, and exudes amber coloured resin. They are vulnerable to frosts in their first three years of age, so they must be protected when young.

Suggested Uses

Formal stately garden tree

Size

From 10 feet high
