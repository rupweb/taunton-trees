Liriodendron Tulipfera - Tulip Tree

Description

A beautiful, large tree with its strange looking leaves turning a rich butter-yellow colour in autumn. The flowers, appearing in summer, are like very short stemmed tulips, yellow green and banded with orange. They are not produced on young trees.

Suggested Uses

For flower gardens

Size

From 2 feet high
