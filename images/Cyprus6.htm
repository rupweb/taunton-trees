Chamaecyparis lawsoniana 'Chilworth Silver' - Lawson's Cyprus

Description

A genus of the cyprus, these trees are a hardy selection of evergreen conifers. This variety is a slow-growing and popular broadly columnular medium-sized bush with silvery blue foliage. Thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Suggested Uses

Ornamental trees of hedging

Size

From 5 to 15 feet high
