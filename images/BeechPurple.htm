Beech Trees

A more detailed description of the Purpurea variety of this noble tree


Fagus Sylvatica 'Purpurea Latifolia' - Purple Beech

Description

This makes a large tree, with large and lively purple leaves in the summer to dark purple in winter. Beech trees are best grown in deep, well-drained soil and sunny position, thriving in acid or alkaline soil.

Suggested Uses

Best used for hedging.

Size

10 - 12 feet high at present, maturing to 100 feet tall.
