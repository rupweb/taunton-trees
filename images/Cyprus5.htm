A more detailed description of this genus of the evergreen coniferous family of trees


Chamaecyparis lawsoniana 'Elwoodii' - Lawson's Cyprus

Description

A genus of the cyprus, these trees are a hardy selection of evergreen conifers. This variety is a slow-growing columnar blue-grey-green and handsome tree. The foliage is densely arranged and changes colour from grey-green in summer to a steel blue in the winter. They are deservedly popular, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.

Suggested Uses

Ornamental trees of hedging

Size

From 5 to 15 feet high
