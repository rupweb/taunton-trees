Magnolia x Soulangeana 'Rustica Rubra' - Magnolia

Description

A vigorous large shrub excellent for general planting with oval leaves and cup-shaped flowers of rich, rosy red. An abundant flower producer.

Suggested Uses

Flower garden tree

Size

From 10 feet high
