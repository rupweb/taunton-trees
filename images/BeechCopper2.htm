A more detailed description of this classic tree


Fagus Sylvatica - Common Beech

Description

This makes a large tree, a light green spring leaf maturing to a forest green in summer, and a rich golden copper in autumn to dark brown in winter. Beech trees are best grown in deep, well-drained soil and sunny position, thriving in acid or alkaline soil.

Suggested Uses

Undoubtedly a noble large tree, also excellent for hedging.

Size

10 - 12 feet high at present, maturing to 100 feet tall
