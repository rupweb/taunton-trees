Sorbus vilmorinii - Chinese Whitebeam

Description

A beautiful small tree with fern-like leaves giving wonderful autumn colour. The berries change from rose-red to pink to pinky-white. This genus is susceptible to the bacterial disease fireblight, so any infected area must be immediately cut out of the tree.

Suggested Uses

For small gardens and autumn colour.

Size

From 14 feet high
