Acer pensylvanicum - Snake Barked Maple

Description

A small tree distinguished for its striated vertical green stem with white streaks.The leaves are large and three-lobed, and turn an attractive bight, rich yellow in autumn.

Suggested Uses

For bark and autumn colour

Size

From 8 feet high
