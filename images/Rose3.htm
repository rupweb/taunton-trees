Rosa rugosa 'Blanc Double de Couvert' - Rose Tree

Description

A strong growing, perpetual flowering deciduous shrub with dense, prickly stems up to 2m high. This Blanc Double de Couvert variety producing the pure and lovely white flowers in amongst the dense thickets of the tree.

Suggested Uses

For hedges and ornamental training

Size

From 2 feet high
