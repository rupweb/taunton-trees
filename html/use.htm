How to Use these Pages



Descriptions

The descriptions on the following pages are matched on typical plants growing in the Priory nurseries. The sizes, shapes and textures of individual shrubs may vary slightly from the text descriptions which serve as a guideline for the whole species.

Orders

To place an order, please telephone or email Taunton Trees and arrange a time to see us. To be sure that someone will be available to serve you, you may request an appointment at a time suitable with you, which we will confirm with you. Please be sure to include an email address or telephone number in your request. Opening hours are by arrangement. We look forward to meeting you in the nursery.
